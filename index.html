  • FEATURES OF EACH PLANET

  1. MERCURY=Mercury is the smallest planet and first planet from sun in our solar system. Mercury is one of the rocky planets. It has no atmosphere, and it doesn't have any moons.

  2. VENUS=Venus is the second planet from the Sun in our solar system.The planet is a little smaller than Earth, and is similar to Earth inside,therefore it is called twin sister of earth.Venus is the brightest object in the sky after the Sun and the Moon looks like a bright star in the morning or evening sky,therefore it is caleedmorning star.It rotates in clockwise direction

  3. EARTH=Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.71% is covered with water,hence it is called blue planet.Earth's atmosphere consists mostly of nitrogen and oxygen.

  4. MARS=Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet.It is the most widely searched planet for life.Mars is called red planet due to presence of iron oxide.

  5. JUPITER=Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest planet in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with a mass one-thousandth that of the Sun, but two-and-a-half times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined. Jupiter is one of the brightest objects visible to the naked eye in the night sky. It is named after Roman god Jupiter. It is also known as "JOVE".

  6. SATURN=Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun. Saturn's atmosphere is made up mostly of hydrogen (H2) and helium (He).Saturn has 53 known moons with an additional 29 moons awaiting confirmation of their discovery.

  7. URANUS=Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun.Uranus is made of water, methane, and ammonia fluids above a small rocky center.Unlike, other planet it rotate in clockwise direction.

  8. NEPTUNE=Neptune is the eighth and farthest-known planet from the Sun. It is third-most-massive planet, and the densest giant planet. It is 17 times the mass of Earth. slightly more massive than its near-twin Uranus. Neptune is denser and physically smaller than Uranus because its greater mass causes more gravitational compression of its atmosphere.

    9. ★NOTE=MERCURY,VENUS,EARTH are called terrestrial planet and others are gaseous planet.
